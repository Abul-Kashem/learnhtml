Experience unparalleled comfort and natural movement with the Rutsu Original Barefoot Sneakers. Designed with a durable canvas upper and a spacious toe box, these sneakers ensure your feet move naturally, promoting better balance and foot health. The ultra-flexible rubber soles provide excellent grip and adaptability for various activities, making them perfect for casual everyday wear.

- Upper Material: Durable canvas. - Toe Box: Spacious design for natural toe spread. - Soles: Ultra-flexible rubber for excellent grip. - Weight: Lightweight construction. - Height: 5.12"/13cm from the ground to the top part of the upper (based on size 8W) - Insoles: Removable for customizable comfort. - Sizes: Available in a range of sizes to ensure a perfect fit. - Color Options: Multiple colors available to suit your style. - Usage: Designed for casual wear.

- Cleaning: Hand wash with mild soap and water. - Drying: Air dry only; do not use a dryer. - Storage: Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. - Maintenance: Remove insoles to clean separately and allow both shoes and insoles to dry completely before reassembling. - Avoid: Do not machine wash, bleach, or iron. Avoid exposing the sneakers to harsh chemicals or extreme temperatures.
